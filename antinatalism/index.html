1 · Concepts & Distinctions 概念与区分

Definition 定义

Antinatalism is the view that procreation is morally wrong, or at minimum that it is not morally good and never required. The term covers a family of positions sharing the conclusion that bringing new sentient beings into existence cannot be justified — by appeal to the interests of those beings, or by appeal to broader consequences, or by appeal to the consent that cannot be given by the non-existent.

The position is not a claim that existing lives should end (that would be promortalism). It is not a claim that one finds one's own life unbearable (that would be a clinical or biographical claim). It is a claim about future sentient beings: that the act of starting a new life cannot be defended on their behalf, because they cannot be benefited by being brought into existence in any sense that survives scrutiny.

反生育主义（antinatalism）主张：生育在道德上是错误的，或至少不是道德上好的，也并非必然之事。这一术语涵盖一组共享同一结论的立场——将新感知主体带入存在，无从辩护；既无法诉诸该主体自身的利益，也无法诉诸更广泛的后果，更无法诉诸「同意」，因为尚未存在者无从同意。

反生育主义并非主张「已经存在的生命应当终结」（那是促死主义，promortalism），也并非「我发觉自己的生命无法忍受」之类的临床或自传陈述。它关乎的是未来的感知主体：开启一段新生命，无法以这段新生命的名义为之辩护——因为在任何经得起审视的意义上，「被带入存在」都谈不上是对它的造益。

Core distinctions 核心区分

Philanthropic vs. misanthropic antinatalism
Benatar's distinction (2015). Philanthropic antinatalism is grounded in concern for the potential being who will be brought into existence: do not procreate because the new life will contain harms that are not adequately offset. Misanthropic antinatalism is grounded in the harms that the new being will inflict on the world: do not procreate because humans cause disproportionate suffering to other humans, animals, and ecosystems. The two arguments are logically independent; either alone is sufficient.
Axiological vs. deontological
Axiological antinatalism claims that coming into existence is, in some evaluative sense, bad (or at least never positively good). Deontological antinatalism claims that the act of procreating violates a moral norm — typically a non-consent or harm-imposition norm — independently of whether the resulting life is on balance good or bad.
Strong vs. weak antinatalism
Strong: it is always morally wrong to procreate. Weak: it is sometimes morally wrong, or there is always a moral reason against procreation that may or may not be overridden by other considerations.
Universal vs. species-relative
Whether the position applies to all sentient procreation or specifically to human procreation. Most analytic antinatalists (Benatar, Cabrera) take universal positions; some environmental antinatalists target humans specifically.
Antinatalism vs. childfree
Personal childfree-by-choice is a lifestyle position. Antinatalism is a normative philosophical claim that others also have reason not to procreate. Not all childfree people are antinatalists; not all antinatalists frame their position as a lifestyle.
仁爱式 vs. 厌世式反生育
出自 Benatar 的区分（2015）。仁爱式（philanthropic）反生育的根据在于对潜在新生命本身的关切：不要生育，因为新生命所含的伤害无法被充分抵偿。厌世式（misanthropic）反生育的根据则在于新生命将施加于世界的伤害：不要生育，因为人类对其他人类、动物及生态系统造成了不成比例的痛苦。两组论证在逻辑上互不依赖，任何一方独立成立即足。
价值论 vs. 义务论
价值论（axiological）反生育认为：在某种评价意义上，被带入存在是坏的（或至少永远算不上积极的好）。义务论（deontological）反生育则认为：生育这一行为本身违反了某项道德规范——通常是「未经同意」或「不可施害」之类的规范——至于由此产生的生命整体是好是坏，与此无关。
强 vs. 弱反生育
强反生育：生育在任何情况下皆为道德错误。弱反生育：生育在某些情况下为错；或者，反对生育的道德理由始终存在，但有可能被其他考量压倒。
普遍 vs. 物种相对
该立场是否适用于一切感知主体的繁殖，抑或专指人类的繁殖。多数分析传统的反生育者（Benatar、Cabrera）持普遍立场；部分环境反生育者则专门针对人类。
反生育主义 vs. 自愿无子
自愿无子（childfree）是一种生活方式的选择；反生育主义则是一项规范性的哲学主张——主张他人也有理由不生育。并非所有自愿无子者都是反生育主义者；反生育主义者亦未必将自己的立场表述为一种生活方式。

The structure of the disagreement 分歧的结构

Disagreement with antinatalism takes three characteristic forms. The first denies the empirical premise that life contains the harms claimed (call this optimist opposition). The second grants the empirical premise but denies the inference from harm-to-some to wrongness-of-procreation, typically by appealing to the goods of existence (call this balance opposition). The third denies that the framework of consent and harm applies to procreation at all, because the potential being has no interests that pre-exist its creation (call this non-identity opposition; see §8).

对反生育主义的反对通常呈三种典型形态。其一是否认其经验前提——否认生命包含它所主张的那些伤害（称为乐观主义式反对）。其二是接受经验前提，但拒绝从「存在伤害」推出「生育为错」，常见做法是诉诸存在所带来的善（称为权衡式反对）。其三是从根本上否认同意与伤害的框架适用于生育——因为潜在主体在被创造之前根本不具备任何利益（称为非同一性式反对；见 §8）。

2 · Historical Lineage 思想史脉络

Antinatalism is older than the term. The thought that it would have been better not to have been born appears in archaic Greek poetry (Theognis, Sophocles), in early Buddhist texts and Hindu renunciate traditions, in Gnostic literature, in Schopenhauer, and recurs through the nineteenth-century philosophical pessimists. The contemporary analytic term antinatalism is usually traced to the South African philosopher David Benatar's 1997 paper "Why It Is Better Never to Come into Existence" and the 2006 book of similar title; the German Antinatalismus was used earlier in continental discussions. The position has both a deep continuous lineage and a recent analytic formalization; the literature does not always recognize how much the latter owes to the former.

反生育主义比这一术语本身古老得多。「不生为最善」之思见于古希腊抒情诗（忒奥格尼斯、索福克勒斯）、早期佛教典籍与印度教遁世传统、诺斯替文献、叔本华，并在十九世纪的悲观主义哲学家中反复回响。当代分析传统中的术语 antinatalism，通常追溯至南非哲学家 David Benatar 1997 年的论文〈为何永不来到存在是更好的〉与 2006 年同名专著；至于德语 Antinatalismus，则早已现身于欧陆讨论。这一立场既承袭一条深远连续的脉络，又经历了一次晚近的分析式形式化——后续文献并不总能意识到，后者从前者那里继承了多少。

Periodization 分期

Ancient (pre-modern). The thought is present but not theorized as a normative claim about procreation specifically. The Silenus saying ("best not to be born, second best to die quickly"), the Buddhist diagnosis of birth as the first of the unsatisfactorinesses (jāti dukkha), Zhuangzi's framing of life as labor and death as rest, and the Gnostic and later Cathar conviction that material generation is the work of a malign or inferior deity — all reach antinatalist conclusions through different ontologies.

Nineteenth-century pessimism. Schopenhauer (1819, 1844) gives the first sustained philosophical argument: the will is a blind insatiable striving; existence is a project of pain only intermittently relieved by boredom; the next generation should not be brought into this. Mainländer (1876) radicalizes Schopenhauer into a metaphysics of God's self-annihilation and recommends voluntary extinction. Leopardi, in Operette morali, treats the same conclusions in a literary register.

Twentieth-century existential and literary. Zapffe ("The Last Messiah," 1933) reframes the problem biologically — humans are equipped with cognitive surplus that detects the meaninglessness their environment offers — and prescribes anchoring, isolation, distraction, and sublimation as the four mechanisms by which the species avoids confronting the conclusion. Cioran (1973) writes the entire genre as aphorism. Ligotti (2010) revives Zapffe for an Anglophone audience.

Contemporary analytic. Benatar (1997, 2006) gives an explicit asymmetry argument cast in possible-worlds and value-theoretic terms. Shiffrin (1999) gives a consent-based argument from harm. Cabrera (2014) gives a negative-ethics version. Overall (2012) and Häyry (2024) develop feminist and policy-oriented versions. The contemporary literature treats the question as a serious topic in normative ethics and population ethics rather than as a literary or temperamental position.

古代（前现代）。这一思想已然存在，但尚未被理论化为针对生育的规范主张。西勒诺斯之言（「不生为最善，速死为次善」）、佛教将「生」列为诸苦之首的判断（jāti dukkha）、庄子以生为劳役、以死为休憩的表述，以及诺斯替派与后世清洁派将物质繁殖视作恶神或低等神之业的信念——这些立场各凭不同的本体论路径，归于同一反生育结论。

十九世纪悲观主义。叔本华（1819、1844）首次给出持续的哲学论证：意志是盲目而无餍的挣扎；存在是一桩痛苦的事业，仅由无聊间或缓解；下一代不应被带入此境。迈兰德（1876）将叔本华激进化为一种「神之自我消亡」的形而上学，进而主张自愿绝灭。莱奥帕尔迪则在《道德小品》中以文学语调推演出同样的结论。

二十世纪存在主义与文学传统。扎普菲（〈最后的弥赛亚〉, 1933）将问题置于生物学框架下重述：人具备过剩的认知能力，足以察觉环境所给予的无意义；他随之将锚定（anchoring）、隔离（isolation）、分心（distraction）、升华（sublimation）列为物种用以回避此察觉的四种机制。齐奥朗（1973）将整个传统化为箴言。Ligotti（2010）则把扎普菲传入英语世界。

当代分析传统。Benatar（1997、2006）在可能世界与价值论框架下给出明确的不对称性论证；Shiffrin（1999）给出基于同意的伤害论证；Cabrera（2014）给出否定伦理学版本；Overall（2012）与 Häyry（2024）则展开女性主义与政策导向的版本。当代文献视此问题为规范伦理学与人口伦理学中的严肃议题，而非仅一种文学或气质性的立场。

3 · Analytic Tradition 分析哲学传统

Schopenhauer · the metaphysical foundation 叔本华 · 形而上学奠基

Die Welt als Wille und Vorstellung (1819, 1844) and the late essays in Parerga und Paralipomena (1851), especially "Über die Nichtigkeit und das Leiden des Lebens" and "Über den Selbstmord," contain the first sustained philosophical argument against procreation. The argument depends on a metaphysical picture: the world as representation is grounded in the world as Will, a single blind insatiable striving that individuates itself into countless centers of need.

The structural argument runs: (i) all willing arises from lack, and lack is suffering; (ii) satisfaction of a want is the cessation of one suffering, immediately replaced either by a new want or, in the rare interval, by boredom — which is itself a refined suffering; (iii) the pendulum between wanting and boredom is the basic form of existence, not an unfortunate accident of it; (iv) the goods of life (pleasure, satisfaction) are negative in character — they consist in the absence of pain — while the evils are positive; (v) therefore an existence is, structurally and on average, an excess of suffering over satisfaction; (vi) to procreate is to inflict such an existence on a new center of will, without that center's consent.

The asymmetry of positive evil and negative good (point iv) is the move that Benatar will later reformulate in possible-worlds terms. Schopenhauer takes himself to be giving an empirical-phenomenological description: this is what willing is, not what some willing happens to be like.

《作为意志和表象的世界》（1819、1844），以及《附录与补遗》（1851）里的晚期诸文——尤其〈论生存的虚无〉与〈论自杀〉——蕴含着第一个持续展开的反生育哲学论证。该论证依托于一幅形而上学图景：作为表象的世界以作为意志的世界为根据；意志单一、盲目、无餍，永在挣扎，并自我个体化为无数需求中心。

论证的结构可概括如下：（i）一切意欲皆源于匮乏，而匮乏即痛苦；（ii）欲求的满足，不过是某一种痛苦的暂时止息，旋即又被新欲求所取代；偶有间隙，则被无聊填补，而无聊本身亦是一种精致的痛苦；（iii）「欲望—无聊」的钟摆便是存在的基本形式，并非其偶然事故；（iv）生命中的「好」（快乐、满足）在性质上是消极的——它们只是痛苦的暂时缺席；而恶则是积极的；（v）故存在在结构上、平均而言，恒以痛苦盈余于满足；（vi）生育，即是将此种存在强加于一个新的意志中心，且该中心无从同意。

其中「积极之恶—消极之善」的不对称性（第 iv 条），正是 Benatar 日后以可能世界框架所重述的那一步。叔本华自视其论为经验—现象学的描述：这是意欲之所是，而非偶然如此。

Benatar · the asymmetry argument Benatar · 不对称性论证

David Benatar's Better Never to Have Been: The Harm of Coming into Existence (Oxford, 2006) and the earlier paper "Why It Is Better Never to Come into Existence" (1997) give the most-cited contemporary argument. The central move is the asymmetry of pleasure and pain.

Consider two scenarios:

  • A. A person exists and undergoes pleasures and pains.
  • B. The same person never exists.

For pain: in (A) pain is bad; in (B) the absence of pain is good — even though there is no one to enjoy the absence. For pleasure: in (A) pleasure is good; in (B) the absence of pleasure is not bad, because there is no one for whom the absence is a deprivation. Hence:

The four-fold asymmetry 四重不对称性
  1. The presence of pain is bad.
  2. The presence of pleasure is good.
  3. The absence of pain is good, even if there is no one to enjoy it.
  4. The absence of pleasure is not bad unless there is someone for whom the absence is a deprivation.

Coming into existence is always a net harm. The good of being is outweighed by the bad of being, while the good of non-being requires no balancing.

来到存在永远是净伤害。存在之善被存在之恶所超过，而非存在之善则无需任何抵偿。

Shiffrin · the consent-based harm argument Shiffrin · 基于同意的伤害论证

Seana Shiffrin, "Wrongful Life, Procreative Responsibility, and the Significance of Harm" (Legal Theory 5, 1999) gives a structurally different argument. Procreation imposes serious, foreseeable, and morally significant harms on a being who cannot consent to them. In ordinary morality, imposing such harms without consent is prima facie wrong, even when the imposition also brings benefits that the agent would (counterfactually, if she existed) endorse.

Shiffrin uses the analogy of the rich neighbor who tosses gold bricks into a sleeping person's yard, breaking their arm in the process. The recipient may end up better off overall, but the act is wrong because (i) the harm was foreseeable, (ii) it was significant, (iii) it was imposed without consent, (iv) there was no pure benefit (no preexisting interest of the recipient that was being served), and (v) the recipient could survive without it. Procreation, Shiffrin argues, shares all five features.

This argument is independent of the asymmetry argument: it does not require that life be on balance bad. It only requires that life contain significant harms and that there is no preexisting subject whose interests demanded the creation.

Seana Shiffrin 在〈生育之过、生育责任与伤害的意义〉（Legal Theory 5, 1999）中给出了一个结构上完全不同的论证。生育，是向一个无从同意的主体施加严重、可预见、且具有道德分量的伤害。按通常道德判断，未经同意而施加此类伤害，初看即为错——纵然此举同时带来了某些「假如该主体已存在便会乐于接受」的益处。

Shiffrin 借用了一则「金砖邻居」的类比：富有的邻居把金砖扔进熟睡者的院子，过程中砸断了对方一条胳膊。受赠者从整体看或许更富有了，但此举仍是错的，理由有五：（i）伤害可预见；（ii）伤害严重；（iii）伤害未经同意；（iv）并不存在纯粹的造益——并无某种先在的主体利益要求此举；（v）受赠者本可在没有此举的情形下安然存活。Shiffrin 论证道：生育恰好同时具备此五项特征。

这一论证独立于不对称性论证：它并不要求生命整体而言是坏的，只要求生命中含有重大伤害，且并无某个先在主体的利益要求此一创造发生。

Cabrera · negative ethics & moral disqualification Cabrera · 否定伦理学与道德资格的失格

Julio Cabrera, in A Critique of Affirmative Morality: A Reflection on Death, Birth and the Value of Life (1989) and Discomfort and Moral Impediment (2014, English 2018), develops a tradition called ética negativa. The central concepts are terminality of being (every being is structured toward death and decay), discomfort (desconforto — the structural friction of being in time, scarcity, and mortality), and moral disqualification (the structural impossibility of acting morally without thereby harming or restricting others).

Cabrera's argument is that to bring a being into existence is to insert it into a condition where genuinely moral action is structurally impossible. Procreation is not merely the imposition of suffering (Benatar) or of harm (Shiffrin); it is the imposition of a condition in which the imposed being cannot avoid wronging others. This adds a deontic dimension absent in pure suffering-based accounts.

Cabrera also defends negative ethics as compatible with engaged and ethically demanding life: one accepts the moral disqualification of one's own existence (one is already here) without compounding it by creating new disqualified existents.

Julio Cabrera 在《对肯定道德的批判：关于死亡、出生与生命价值的反思》（1989）以及《不适与道德障碍》（2014；英译 2018）中，发展出一脉名为否定伦理学（ética negativa）的传统。核心概念包括：存在的终结性（每一存在在结构上皆趋向死亡与衰朽）；不适（desconforto——人身处时间、稀缺与有死性之中所必然承受的结构性摩擦）；以及道德资格的失格（moral disqualification——「在不伤害、不限制他人的前提下展开真正的道德行动」之所以在结构上不可能）。

Cabrera 的论证是：把一个存在者带入存在，便是将其置入一种「真正道德的行动在结构上不可能」的处境之中。生育不仅是施加痛苦（Benatar）或施加伤害（Shiffrin），更是施加一种处境——使被施加者无法避免伤害他人。此一义务论维度，是纯粹基于痛苦的进路所欠缺的。

Cabrera 同时主张：否定伦理学与「积极投入、伦理要求严格的生活」相容——人尽可接受自身存在的道德失格（既已身在此处），而不必再通过创造新的失格存在者来累加这种失格。

Other analytic contributions 其他分析传统贡献

  • Gerald Harrison & Julia Tanner ("Better Not to Have Children," 2011) — a risk-based argument: even if life is usually worth living, the probability of a seriously bad life is high enough that imposing the risk without consent is wrong.
  • Asheel Singh ("Assessing Anti-Natalism," 2018) — taxonomy of antinatalist arguments and assessment of which premises do the work.
  • Nicholas Smyth — argues that Benatar's asymmetry can be defended by appeal to the intuitive moral significance of agent-relative reasons.
  • Rivka Weinberg (The Risk of a Lifetime, 2015) — develops a contractualist analogue: procreation is permissible only if the prospective parent could justify the act to the future child under reasonable standards of risk-imposition. Reaches a partial pro-natalist conclusion, but the framework is widely cited by antinatalists.
  • Gerald Harrison 与 Julia Tanner（〈不要生育更好〉, 2011）——基于风险的论证：纵使一般生命值得活，「严重不幸的生命」之发生率仍然足够高，因此未经同意便施加此种风险，便已是错。
  • Asheel Singh（〈评估反生育主义〉, 2018）——对反生育论证作分类学梳理，并分析究竟哪些前提承担着论证的实质重量。
  • Nicholas Smyth——主张可借助「主体相对理由」（agent-relative reasons）所具的直觉性道德分量，为 Benatar 的不对称性辩护。
  • Rivka Weinberg（《一生的风险》, 2015）——发展出一套契约主义类比：唯有当准父母能依据合理的风险施加标准，向未来的孩子作出辩护时，生育才是被允许的。其结论在某种程度上偏向支持生育，但所建框架仍被反生育文献广泛援引。

4 · Literary & Pessimist Tradition 文学与悲观主义传统

The literary tradition reaches antinatalist conclusions by different means than the analytic. Where the analytic argues from premises about harm, consent, or value, the literary shows the structural features of existence — boredom, repetition, decay, the dissolution of love, the betrayals of memory, the futility of effort — and leaves the reader to draw the conclusion. The form is sometimes more powerful than the argument because the form rules out the easy reply ("you are exaggerating") by sheer accumulation of accurate detail.

文学传统抵达反生育结论的路径，与分析传统不同。分析传统从「伤害」「同意」「价值」之类的前提推演，而文学传统则呈现存在的结构性特征——无聊、重复、衰朽、爱的瓦解、记忆的背叛、努力的徒劳——然后把结论留给读者自行得出。这种形式有时比论证更具说服力，因为它以精确细节的纯然累积，封堵了「你只是夸大其词」之类的轻易回应。

Silenus & the Greek lyric 西勒诺斯与希腊抒情诗

The saying attributed to Silenus, preserved in Aristotle's lost Eudemus and in Plutarch's Consolatio ad Apollonium: "Best of all for mortal beings is never to have been born at all; second-best is to die as soon as possible." Nietzsche cites this in The Birth of Tragedy §3 as the wisdom that Greek tragedy was constructed to mask. Whether or not the saying represents an "old" Greek view or a post-Platonic literary construction, it became a topos. Theognis of Megara (6th c. BCE) versifies the same thought in elegies 425–428.

Sophocles, Oedipus at Colonus 1224–1238 (the epigraph to this page) gives the most often-quoted form. The Greek phrase mē phūnai ("not to be born") becomes a stock element in later pessimist citation. Hegesias of Cyrene ("the death-persuader," 3rd c. BCE) reportedly argued that life contains more pain than pleasure and that one should welcome death; Ptolemy II is said to have banned his teaching in Alexandria after a wave of suicides.

归在西勒诺斯名下的一句，见于亚里士多德佚作《欧德谟》及普鲁塔克《致阿波罗尼乌斯的慰文》：「对凡人而言，至善是从未出生；次善是尽快死去。」尼采在《悲剧的诞生》§3 中援引此言，称之为希腊悲剧所致力遮蔽的智慧。无论它代表的是「古老」希腊观念，抑或一种后柏拉图式的文学构造，它早已成为一个不断被回响的母题（topos）。忒奥格尼斯（公元前 6 世纪）在哀歌 425–428 行中亦以韵语表达过同一念头。

索福克勒斯《俄狄浦斯在科罗诺斯》1224–1238 行（本页题词所在），给出了此一思想最常被引用的形态。希腊语短语 mē phūnai（「未曾被生」）从此成为后世悲观主义引文中的固定意象。昔兰尼的赫格西亚（公元前 3 世纪，时人称之为「劝死者」），据说论证生命中痛苦多于快乐，故应当从容迎死；据传亚历山大里亚因其讲学而引发一波自杀潮，托勒密二世遂将其禁绝。

Leopardi · the cosmic indifference 莱奥帕尔迪 · 宇宙的冷漠

Giacomo Leopardi (1798–1837), particularly Operette morali (1827) and the Zibaldone, develops a philosophical pessimism that approaches antinatalism through the doctrine of infelicità necessaria (necessary unhappiness). The "Dialogue of Nature and an Icelander" stages Nature as an indifferent productive force that creates beings whose only function is, in their living and dying, to maintain the cosmic cycle — without any concern for their welfare. Reproduction, in this view, is not the propagation of any good but the perpetuation of the same indifferent process.

Leopardi distinguishes storical pessimism (a critique of his own age) from cosmic pessimism (a structural account of existence as such); only the latter has antinatalist implications.

Giacomo Leopardi（1798–1837），尤以《道德小品》（1827）与《杂记》（Zibaldone）为代表，发展出一种通过必然不幸（infelicità necessaria）学说而逼近反生育主义的哲学悲观主义。〈自然与冰岛人的对话〉将「自然」描绘为一种漠然的生产力——它造出一切存在者，使其在生死之间维系宇宙循环，全然不顾各别的福祉。从这一视角看，生育并非任何「善」的繁衍，而只是同一漠然过程的延续。

Leopardi 区分了历史性悲观主义（对自身时代的批判）与宇宙性悲观主义（对存在本身的结构性陈述）；具有反生育意涵的，唯有后者。

Mainländer · the will to nothingness 迈兰德 · 趋向虚无的意志

Philipp Mainländer (1841–1876), Die Philosophie der Erlösung (1876) radicalizes Schopenhauer. The metaphysical move is daring: God existed but, finding existence intolerable, chose self-annihilation. The Big Bang is the moment of this self-annihilation; the universe is the slow decay of God's fragments. Each individual existent therefore contains, as its deepest tendency, a will to die — a will to return to the divine nothingness. Reproduction prolongs the decay; voluntary non-reproduction accelerates the return.

Mainländer is also notable for the biographical coda: he completed Die Philosophie der Erlösung in 1876, received the printed first volume, and hanged himself the same night using the stacked copies as a platform. He cannot be cited without this fact; the question of whether the fact strengthens or weakens his argument is unresolved.

Philipp Mainländer（1841–1876）在《救赎的哲学》（1876）中，将叔本华激进化。其形而上学的步法极为大胆：神曾经存在，但发现存在无法忍受，遂选择自我消亡。大爆炸即此一自我消亡的时刻；宇宙则是神之碎片的缓慢衰朽。因此，每一个体存在者，其最深的倾向中皆含有一种趋死的意志——一种归返神之虚无的意志。生育延长此种衰朽；自愿不生育则加速此种归返。

Mainländer 还以一段著名的传记结尾闻名：他于 1876 年完成《救赎的哲学》，收到第一卷的印本，同夜便以这一摞印本为台座，自缢而亡。引述他时无法回避此事；而这一事实究竟是强化还是削弱了他的论证，至今悬而未决。

Zapffe · the four mechanisms 扎普菲 · 四种机制

Peter Wessel Zapffe (1899–1990), "The Last Messiah" (Den sidste Messias, 1933) and On the Tragic (Om det tragiske, 1941) reframes the antinatalist conclusion through a biological asymmetry. Humans evolved cognitive capacities — for self-awareness, anticipation of death, moral consideration of others — that exceed the meanings their evolutionary environment can supply. The result is a structural mismatch: humans are equipped to recognize the meaninglessness of their condition but unequipped to live with that recognition.

Zapffe identifies four mechanisms by which the species defends itself against this recognition:

1. Anchoring (forankring)
Fixing attention on social roles, institutions, ideologies, gods, nations — frameworks that supply borrowed meaning. The frameworks are perceived as natural rather than as defenses.
2. Isolation (isolasjon)
Excluding disturbing thoughts from awareness. The conclusions of pessimist philosophy are tolerated as ideas but not allowed to bear on daily decisions.
3. Distraction (distraksjon)
Continuous occupation of attention with stimuli — entertainment, work, ambition, romance — that prevent the recognition from rising. The function of an information environment is, in part, to make introspection costly.
4. Sublimation (sublimasjon)
Transforming the recognition into aesthetic form — tragedy, lyric poetry, philosophy. This is the only mechanism that does not deny the recognition; it converts it into something the species can survive contemplating. The text you are reading is itself an instance.

The "Last Messiah" of the title is the figure who would tell humans the truth that they should stop reproducing. Zapffe predicts that such a messiah would be killed or, more likely, simply ignored.

Peter Wessel Zapffe（1899–1990）在〈最后的弥赛亚〉（Den sidste Messias, 1933）与《论悲剧》（Om det tragiske, 1941）中，把反生育结论置于一种生物学不对称性之中重述。人类演化出过剩的认知能力——自我意识、对死亡的预期、对他人的道德考量——这些能力远远超出了其演化环境所能赋予的意义。由此产生一种结构性错配：人类既被装备得足以察觉自身处境的无意义，又未被装备得足以与这一察觉共存。

Zapffe 列出物种用以抵御此种察觉的四种机制：

一、锚定（forankring）
将注意力系于社会角色、制度、意识形态、神祇、民族等等借来的意义框架。这些框架被当作天经地义，而非被识别为防御机制本身。
二、隔离（isolasjon）
把令人不安的念头屏蔽在意识之外。悲观主义哲学的结论被允许以「观念」身份存在，但不容许它们左右日常决策。
三、分心（distraksjon）
以连续不绝的刺激——娱乐、工作、抱负、爱情——占据注意力，使「察觉」无暇浮现。信息环境的功能之一，正是让内省变得代价高昂。
四、升华（sublimasjon）
把察觉转换为美学形式——悲剧、抒情诗、哲学。这是唯一不否认察觉的机制；它将察觉转化为「物种凝视之而不致溃散」之物。你眼下正在阅读的这段文字本身，便是一个实例。

篇题中的「最后的弥赛亚」，正是那位本应向人类宣告「应当停止繁殖」之真理者。Zapffe 预言：这样一位弥赛亚要么被杀害，要么——更有可能——只是被无声地忽视。

Cioran · the aphorist of nativity 齐奥朗 · 出生的箴言家

Emil Cioran (1911–1995), De l'inconvénient d'être né (The Trouble with Being Born, 1973) is the antinatalist position rendered entirely as aphorism. Cioran does not argue; he formulates the recognition in the most compressed possible verbal forms and repeats it until accumulation does the work of demonstration. Representative claim: birth is a fall not from grace but from possibility; we existed potentially in countless better ways, and existing actually in this one way is the loss of all of them.

Cioran is also distinctive for not framing the position morally. He does not say procreation is wrong; he says being born is unfortunate, and he leaves the inference to procreation as an exercise. This rhetorical move is sometimes more effective than the explicit moral claim because it makes the conclusion harder to dismiss as moralism.

Emil Cioran（1911–1995）在《诞生之不便》（De l'inconvénient d'être né, 1973）中，把反生育立场彻底转译为箴言。Cioran 并不论证；他将「察觉」浓缩为最简的语言形态，并以反复重申让累积本身代替证明。其代表性陈述大抵如此：出生并非从恩典中的堕落，而是从可能性中的堕落——我们本可在无数更好的样态中潜在地存在，而仅以这唯一一种样态实存，便是失却了其余所有。

Cioran 之独到，还在于他不把这一立场道德化。他不说生育为错；他只说被生即不幸，至于由此推及生育的那一步，则作为练习留给读者。这一修辞策略有时比明示的道德主张更有效——因为它使结论难以被作为「道德说教」轻巧打发。

Ligotti · horror as philosophy Ligotti · 作为哲学的恐怖

Thomas Ligotti, The Conspiracy against the Human Race (2010), is a non-fiction work by an American horror writer that systematizes Zapffe, Schopenhauer, and Mainländer for an Anglophone readership. The central thesis is that consciousness is a malignant accident — that to be a conscious being is to be a piece of inert matter that has accidentally acquired the capacity to recognize what it is, and that horror fiction is the literary form most honest about this recognition. The cosmic-horror tradition (Lovecraft, Chambers, Machen) is read as antinatalism in genre code.

Thomas Ligotti 的《针对人类的阴谋》（The Conspiracy against the Human Race, 2010），是这位美国恐怖小说作家所撰的一部非虚构作品，将 Zapffe、叔本华与 Mainländer 为英语读者系统地接引过来。其核心论点是：意识是一种恶性的偶然——所谓有意识的存在者，无非是一团惰性物质，偶然获得了「认识自身所是」的能力；而恐怖文学，则是面对此一察觉最诚实的文学形式。宇宙恐怖传统（洛夫克拉夫特、钱伯斯、马钦）于是被解作类型化编码下的反生育主义。

5 · Eastern Traditions 东方传统

The Eastern traditions reach antinatalist conclusions through different metaphysical frames — most commonly through a soteriology in which birth itself is the condition to be escaped, and reproduction is the act that perpetuates the bondage. The conclusions overlap with Western antinatalism but the argumentative routes are different; conflation flattens both.

东方传统经由不同的形而上学框架抵达反生育的结论——最常见的是一种救赎论：「生」本身即是应当脱离的境况，而「繁衍」便是延续此一束缚的行为。其结论与西方反生育主义重合，但论证路径各异；若将二者混同，反而把双方都磨平了。

Buddhism · birth as the first dukkha 佛教 · 生为初苦

The Four Noble Truths open with the enumeration of dukkha (often translated "suffering" but more accurately "unsatisfactoriness," asantosa). The standard formula begins: jāti pi dukkhā — "birth is dukkha." The list continues: aging is dukkha, illness is dukkha, death is dukkha, encountering what is unpleasant is dukkha, separation from what is pleasant is dukkha, not getting what one wants is dukkha; in short, the five aggregates of clinging are dukkha. Birth is not one item among others; it is the condition from which the others follow.

The goal of the Buddhist path is the cessation of rebirth (punabbhava-nirodha): not death of the present body, which is merely transition, but the breaking of the cycle that produces new births. Within Pāli Buddhism, ajāta ("the unborn") is one of the canonical epithets of nibbāna. The position is structurally antinatalist at the soteriological level: the goal is the cessation of new births. Whether this implies a normative claim against ordinary procreation is debated within the tradition; monastic life (which implies non-procreation) is one resolution.

The Mahāyāna development complicates this. The Bodhisattva vow — to remain in saṃsāra until all beings are liberated — converts non-rebirth from a goal into a deferred goal. But the underlying valuation of jāti as a condition to be escaped is preserved.

四圣谛以对「苦」（dukkha，通常译作「苦」，更精确则为「不满足」，asantosa）的列举开篇。标准表述自：jāti pi dukkhā——「生是苦」——而起。其后续为：老是苦，病是苦，死是苦，怨憎会是苦，爱别离是苦，求不得是苦；总而言之，五取蕴是苦。「生」并非众苦中的一项，而是其余诸苦皆由之而生的根本境况。

佛教修行的目标，是「再生之止息」（punabbhava-nirodha）——这并不是当下肉身的死亡（死亡只是流转），而是让那个不断产生新生的循环就此断绝。在巴利佛教中，ajāta（「不生」）正是涅槃的经典称名之一。从救赎论的层面看，此一立场在结构上即是反生育的：目标即是新生的止息。至于这是否蕴含一项针对日常生育的规范主张，传统内部尚有争论；僧团生活（其前提便是不生育）便是其中一种解答。

大乘佛教的发展使问题更为微妙。菩萨之愿——长住轮回，直至众生悉皆解脱——把「不再生」从一项当下目标改造为一项延期的目标；但「jāti 乃是应当脱离之境」这一根本价值评定，仍被保留。

Hindu renunciate & Jain traditions 印度教遁世传统与耆那教

The renunciate (saṃnyāsa) tradition in Hinduism treats withdrawal from procreation and household life as the final and highest of the four life stages (āśramas). The pattern is biographical-developmental rather than antinatalist as a universal claim: householders are expected to procreate (the stage of gṛhastha); only the post-householder is expected to renounce. The valuation, however, places non-procreation higher.

Jain ethics is more directly antinatalist in its implications. Strict ahiṃsā (non-violence) extends to all sentient life, including the microscopic organisms in food and water; the logical endpoint of perfect ahiṃsā is sallekhanā, the ritual fast unto death. Reproduction is the production of more beings who will inevitably commit himsa. Jain monastic celibacy is therefore not only continence but doctrinal non-procreation.

印度教的遁世（saṃnyāsa）传统，将「从生育与家庭生活中退出」置于人生四阶段（āśramas）中最末、最高的位置。这一框架属于传记—发展型的：家主期（gṛhastha）的人被期待生育；唯有走出家主期之后，方被期待出家。因此它并不构成一项普遍意义上的反生育主张——但在其价值序列里，不生育仍居于更高的位置。

耆那教的伦理学，在蕴含上则更直接地走向反生育。严格的「不害」（ahiṃsā）延及一切有情，包括饮食水中的微生物；完美不害的逻辑终点正是sallekhanā，即斋戒至死的仪式。生育，便是制造更多必将施害的存在者。因此，耆那教僧团的禁欲不只是节制肉身，更是教义层面对生育的拒绝。

Zhuangzi · life as labor, death as rest 庄子 · 生为劳役，死为休憩

Zhuangzi 6 (Dazongshi 大宗师): "大块载我以形，劳我以生，佚我以老，息我以死。故善吾生者，乃所以善吾死也。" ("The Great Lump burdens me with form, labors me with life, eases me with old age, and rests me with death. Therefore, that which makes my life good is the same that makes my death good.") The framing treats existence as labor and death as relief. The implication is not a strict normative claim against procreation, but the evaluative orientation of the whole tradition — that the wise person does not cling to life, that distinctions between life and death are perspectival, that "having been born" is a contingency one need not particularly celebrate — is antinatalist-adjacent.

Zhuangzi 18 (Zhile 至乐) contains the famous episode of Zhuangzi singing while his wife is mourned: the framing is not lack of feeling but recognition that the transformation she has undergone is a return to the same cosmic process from which she briefly individuated. Both birth and death are transitions within a cycle that has no preferred state.

《庄子·大宗师》有云：「大块载我以形，劳我以生，佚我以老，息我以死。故善吾生者，乃所以善吾死也。」此中将存在视为劳役，将死亡视为休止。此处的意味并非对生育下一道严格的规范禁令，但整个传统的评价取向——智者不执着于生、生死之分原是视角性的、「曾被生」不过是无须特意庆贺的偶然——已与反生育主义相邻。

《庄子·至乐》载有庄子鼓盆而歌的著名一段：所传达的并非冷漠无情，而是认知到其妻所经历的转化，不过是回归她曾短暂个体化而出的同一宇宙过程。生与死同样只是流转，并非两种有偏好之别的状态。

Honglou Meng & the dream of vanity 《红楼梦》与一梦荒唐

The opening chapter of Honglou Meng (Cao Xueqin, mid-18th c.) supplies the framework for the entire novel: the "Hao Liao Ge" (好了歌) and Zhen Shiyin's gloss on it. The pattern is that every form of human striving — wealth, love, status, scholarly achievement, even the parental relation — is shown across time to dissolve into vanity. The famous closing lines of Shiyin's gloss: "乱烘烘你方唱罢我登场，反认他乡是故乡。甚荒唐，到头来都是为他人作嫁衣裳。" ("In a noisy confusion you finish singing and I take the stage; we mistake the foreign place for home. How absurd — in the end, we are all sewing wedding clothes for others.")

The frame is Buddhist–Daoist: the world of forms is impermanent and ultimately empty; attachment to any of its objects, including descendants, is the engine of suffering. The poem at the end of the first chapter — "满纸荒唐言，一把辛酸泪。都云作者痴，谁解其中味。" ("Pages of absurd words, a handful of bitter tears. They all say the author is mad; who understands the taste within?") — frames the entire 120-chapter narrative as evidence for the conclusion. The narrative is not an argument that procreation is wrong; it is a 800,000-character demonstration that the goods one would procreate for are not what they appear.

The line that supplies this wiki's title — "悲喜千般同幻渺，古今一梦尽荒唐" ("a thousand sorrows and joys all alike are phantoms; past and present alike, a single dream of absurdity") — is from the closing poem of Chapter 1, placed by the author as the work's epistemic frame. The English half of the title (A Dream Not Recommended) joins the second half of this couplet to a private skiing analogy (see §8, the "why not suicide" reply).

《红楼梦》（曹雪芹，十八世纪中叶）开篇即为整部小说设下框架：〈好了歌〉与甄士隐之注。其模式如此：人世一切奋斗——财富、爱情、地位、学问，乃至亲子之伦——在时间中皆终归虚空。士隐之注的著名收束句：「乱烘烘你方唱罢我登场，反认他乡是故乡。甚荒唐，到头来都是为他人作嫁衣裳。」

这一框架是佛家与道家共同的：形相之世无常而终空；对其任何对象的执着，包括对子嗣的执着，便是苦的发动机。第一回末诗——「满纸荒唐言，一把辛酸泪。都云作者痴，谁解其中味。」——则将其后一百二十回的整部叙事，置于此结论的注脚之列。叙事本身并非「生育为错」的论证；它是一次以约八十万字铺展开来的展示：人为之而生育的那些「好」，并不真是它们所看上去的样子。

本页标题中的「荒唐一梦」即取自「悲喜千般同幻渺，古今一梦尽荒唐」一句——此句出自第一回末诗，由作者亲置以为全作的认识论框架。题目的英文部分（A Dream Not Recommended）则将此句的下半句，与一则私人的滑雪类比相缀接（见 §8「为何不自杀」一节的回应）。

Tao Yuanming & the return to nothing 陶渊明 · 归于空无

Tao Yuanming (365–427) gives the framing in lyric form. The most cited line: "人生似幻化，终当归空无" (《归园田居·其四》) — "Human life is like an apparition, in the end returning to emptiness." The framing is more Daoist than Buddhist (Tao predates Chinese Buddhism's full reception); the conclusion is the same.

陶渊明（365–427）以抒情诗的形式给出同一框架。最常被引用之句：「人生似幻化，终当归空无」（《归园田居·其四》）。其根基近道家而非佛家（陶活动的年代尚早于汉地佛教的完整接受），但结论无异。

6 · Feminist Antinatalism 女性主义反生育

The feminist contribution to antinatalism is distinct in three ways. First, it shifts the burden of proof: rather than asking whether refusing to procreate requires justification, it asks whether procreating requires justification — a reversal that fits poorly with the dominant cultural framing but well with ordinary moral reasoning about other significant actions. Second, it foregrounds the asymmetric burdens of procreation on women's lives, bodies, and labor. Third, it engages directly with the policy and rights questions (reproductive autonomy, pronatalist incentives, climate-driven reproductive ethics) that the analytic tradition often brackets.

女性主义对反生育主义的贡献，可从三方面与他者区别。其一，它翻转了举证的责任：与其问「拒绝生育是否需要辩护」，不如问生育本身是否需要辩护——这一翻转与当下主流的文化框架不合，却恰好契合我们处理其他重大行为时的通常道德推理。其二，它将生育对女性生活、身体与劳动所造成的不对称负担置于显眼的位置。其三，它直接介入政策与权利层面的议题——生育自主权、亲生育激励、气候压力下的生殖伦理——而这些恰是分析传统时常搁置不论的部分。

Overall · the burden of proof reversed Overall · 举证责任的翻转

Christine Overall, Why Have Children? The Ethical Debate (MIT Press, 2012), opens with the observation that the question is asked only of the childfree. Those who have children are not normally expected to explain themselves; those who do not are. Overall argues that this distribution of the explanatory burden is unjustifiable: bringing a new sentient being into existence is by any measure a more consequential action than not doing so, and ordinary moral reasoning places the burden of justification on the more consequential side.

Overall does not argue for strong antinatalism. Her conclusion is a procedural reversal: procreation requires positive moral reasons; non-procreation requires none. She also argues that the strongest pro-natalist reasons (continuation of family lineage, social expectation, parental fulfillment) do not survive examination when stated explicitly.

Christine Overall 的《为何要有孩子？伦理之辩》（MIT, 2012）开篇即点明一事：这一问题，唯独被向无子者提出。有子者通常不被期待解释自己；唯有无子者才被期待。Overall 论证说：这种「解释负担」的分配方式本身经不起辩护——把一个新感知主体带入存在，无论以什么标准衡量，都比「不去做」更为重大；而通常的道德推理把辩护责任置于更重大的那一边。

Overall 并未主张强反生育主义。她得出的结论只是一项程序上的翻转：生育需要拿出积极的道德理由；不生育则无须任何理由。她同时论证：那些最有力的「亲生育」理由——延续家族、应符社会期待、为人父母带来的满足——一旦被明明白白地陈述出来，便都经不起细察。

Häyry · procreative permissibility Häyry · 生育的可容许性

Matti Häyry, in If You Must Make Babies (Cambridge, 2024) and earlier work, develops a structured framework for thinking about procreative permissibility. The framework distinguishes five positions on a continuum from pronatalism (procreation is good) through permissive and cautious views to restrictive antinatalism (procreation is wrong in most cases) and finally strict antinatalism (procreation is always wrong). Häyry argues that the cautious-to-restrictive positions are best supported on independently plausible moral premises.

The book is also methodologically distinctive: rather than arguing for one position, it provides a structured map and shows what each position commits its proponents to. This serves the policy purpose well — it makes visible the choices buried in pronatalist defaults.

Matti Häyry 在《如果你必须要生孩子》（剑桥, 2024）及更早期的著作中，发展出一套关于「生育可容许性」的结构化框架。该框架在一条连续谱上区分五种立场：亲生育（生育为善）；容许性立场；谨慎立场；限制性反生育（多数情况下生育为错）；严格反生育（生育始终为错）。Häyry 论证道：在一些独立而可信的道德前提下，从「谨慎」到「限制性反生育」一段获得了最坚实的支持。

这本书在方法论上也颇具特色：它并不直接为某一立场辩护，而是给出一张结构化的地图，并清楚标示出每一立场对其支持者意味着什么承诺。这一做法对政策讨论尤为有用——它使得隐藏在「亲生育默认」之下的种种选择，得以浮出水面。

7 · Empirical Background: Why Parenthood Is the Default & Why TFR Is Falling 经验背景：为何「为人父母」是默认人生轨迹，以及全球总和生育率为何下降

The normative arguments in §§3–6 take place against a social fact: in most cultures and most periods, procreation has been the default life trajectory, and the burden of justification has fallen on those who deviate from it. The descriptive question — why this is so — is logically separable from the normative question, but the two interact: the social fact shapes the moral intuitions on which the normative arguments draw, and the recent reversal of the fact (rapid TFR decline) provides an empirical setting in which the normative arguments are received differently than they would have been a century ago. This section sets out the descriptive background.

§§3–6 的规范论证发生在一个社会事实的背景之上：在大多数文化、大多数时期，生育曾是默认的人生轨迹；辩护责任落于偏离这一默认者的身上。「为何如此」是一项描述性问题，逻辑上可与规范问题分立；但两者其实彼此渗透——社会事实塑造着规范论证所凭借的道德直觉；而该事实近来的反转——全球总和生育率（TFR）的迅速下降——也使得相同的规范论证，在今日所处的接收语境，已与一百年前大为不同。本节梳理的便是这一描述性背景。

Why parenthood is the default: pronatalist ideology 为何「为人父母」是默认：亲生育意识形态

The sociological literature names the default pronatalism: the family of beliefs, practices, and institutional arrangements that treat procreation as the normal, valuable, and expected adult activity, and treat non-procreation as a deviation requiring explanation. The term traces to demographic policy literature of the early twentieth century but was developed as a critical concept by feminist sociology beginning in the 1970s. Veevers (1975) and later Park (2002) document the patterns of stigma management among the voluntarily childless; Meyers (2001) identifies two cultural mechanisms that sustain the norm — the glorification of motherhood (the idealization of parenthood's emotional rewards while difficulties are minimized) and the matrigyno-idolatry (the conflation of female value with reproductive capacity). Fikslin (2021) reviews the contemporary psychological literature and shows that pronatalist norms operate across cultural, ideological, psychological, and political levels simultaneously.

Four structural mechanisms recur in the sociological accounts.

1. Institutional reinforcement
Marriage, family law, religious doctrine, tax systems, housing markets, and welfare arrangements have historically been organized around the expectation of child-rearing households. Even where individual institutions have liberalized (e.g., legal recognition of childfree marriage), the institutional ecosystem still treats parenthood as the unmarked case.
2. State pronatalism
As of 2019, 28 per cent of UN member states reported policies explicitly aimed at raising fertility — up from 20 per cent in 2005. Examples include South Korea's child allowances, Hungary's housing-loan forgiveness conditional on children, France's quotient familial, and Italy's assegno unico. The increase tracks the spread of below-replacement fertility, which states interpret as a crisis requiring intervention rather than as an aggregate outcome of individual choices.
3. The asymmetric burden of explanation
Overall's observation (§6) operationalized: in survey research, those without children are more often asked why and more often provide justifications than those with children. The default does not need to argue for itself; deviations from it do. This asymmetry is itself a marker of normative force, not a neutral feature of the conversation.
4. Psychological mechanisms
Zapffe's four mechanisms (anchoring, isolation, distraction, sublimation; see §4) apply to procreative decision-making. The structure of a procreative life provides anchoring (a multi-decade project with built-in milestones), isolation (the urgency of parenting tasks crowds out reflection on whether to have undertaken them), and distraction. Carroll (2012), summarizing the sociological literature: "very few [people] ponder if parenthood is desirable or that it befits their lifestyle." The non-decision is the modal case.

The four mechanisms are mutually reinforcing. A young adult considering procreation is rarely conducting an independent calculation; the calculation has been pre-structured by institutions, partially subsidized by state policy, treated as the default in conversational defaults, and supplied with a ready-made life-project structure. The Overall (§6) reversal of the explanatory burden, on this analysis, is not a rhetorical move but a corrective to a documented structural asymmetry.

It is sometimes argued that the procreative impulse is biologically innate and therefore not in need of cultural explanation. This argument confuses two questions. That sexual desire and pair-bonding are evolved is well-supported; that the decision to bear and raise a child is the same kind of biological reflex is not. Veenhoven (1974), reviewing the cross-cultural variation, concludes that "the desire to procreate ... is not necessarily instinctive." The default-ness of procreation is a cultural fact partially constructed on a biological substrate, not a direct readout of biology.

社会学文献把这一默认状态称作亲生育主义（pronatalism）：一组将生育视为正常、有价值且应当被期待的成人活动，并将不生育视为需要解释的偏离的信念、实践与制度安排。这一术语可追溯至二十世纪初的人口政策文献，但其作为一项批判性概念，是 1970 年代起的女性主义社会学逐步发展出来的。Veevers（1975）以及稍后的 Park（2002）记录了自愿无子女者管理污名的种种模式；Meyers（2001）则点出维持此种规范的两条文化机制——母职的神化（理想化为人父母的情感回报、淡化其中的艰辛）与母性—女性偶像化（把女性的价值等同于她的生育能力）。Fikslin（2021）综述当代心理学文献后指出，亲生育规范同时在文化、意识形态、心理与政治四个层面上运作。

社会学论述中反复浮现的，是以下四种结构性机制。

一、制度的强化
婚姻、家庭法、宗教教义、税收体系、住房市场、福利安排——历来皆围绕「养育子女的家庭」这一预期而组织起来。即便其中某些制度已经自由化（譬如对自愿无子婚姻给予法律承认），整套制度生态依旧将「为人父母」视作无标记的常态。
二、国家层面的亲生育政策
截至 2019 年，联合国成员国中已有 28% 报告其设有明确旨在提高生育率的政策，相比 2005 年的 20% 有所上升。例如：韩国的育儿津贴、匈牙利按子女数核减住房贷款的方案、法国的家庭份额（quotient familial）、意大利的单一津贴（assegno unico）。这一上升与「低于更替水平」生育现象的扩散同步——国家通常将后者解读为有待干预的危机，而非个体选择的总合结果。
三、解释负担的不对称
这是 Overall（§6）那一观察的实证体现：在调查研究中，无子女者远比有子女者更常被追问理由、也更频繁地为自己作出辩护。默认本身无须自证；偏离默认者才需要。这种不对称本身便是规范力量的印记，而非中性的对话特征。
四、心理层面的机制
Zapffe 所提出的四种机制（锚定、隔离、分心、升华，见 §4），同样适用于生育决策。养育生活的结构本身便提供了锚定（一个内建着各种里程碑、长达数十年的项目）、隔离（养育任务的紧迫性挤压了「是否当初就不该承担」之类的反思空间），以及分心。Carroll（2012）综述这一脉社会学文献时直言：「极少有人会去考虑，为人父母究竟是否值得、是否真的契合自己的生活方式。」不做决定，才是常态。

这四种机制彼此加强。一个正在考虑生育的年轻人，极少真正在做独立的计算；他/她所面对的「计算」，早已被制度预先结构化、被国家政策局部补贴、被对话场域默认为基线，且配套有一整套现成的人生项目模板。如此看来，Overall（§6）对解释负担的翻转，并非修辞上的姿态，而是对一项有据可考的结构性不对称所作的纠偏。

有时有人主张：生育冲动出自生物本能，因此无须文化层面的解释。此论混淆了两个问题。性欲与配偶结合是演化的产物，证据充分；但生养一个孩子的决定是否同样属于生物反射，则无足够证据支持。Veenhoven（1974）综述跨文化变异后得出的结论是：「生育的欲望……未必是本能。」「生育是默认」是一个部分建立在生物基底上的文化事实，而非生物学直接给出的输出。

The proximate determinants framework 近端决定因素框架

The standard demographic framework for explaining variation in fertility levels is the proximate determinants model developed by Davis and Blake (1956) and operationalized in numerical form by Bongaarts (1978, 2015). The framework distinguishes distal determinants (socio-economic conditions, education, cultural norms) from proximate determinants (the biological-behavioral mechanisms through which any distal cause must operate). The original eight proximate determinants were reduced to four that account for approximately 96 per cent of cross-national variation in TFR:

  1. Proportion of women in sexual union (essentially: age at first marriage / cohabitation, divorce rates, never-married rates)
  2. Contraceptive use and effectiveness
  3. Induced abortion (where legal access exists and is exercised)
  4. Postpartum infecundability (mainly a function of breastfeeding duration)

The framework is a useful clarifying device because it forces the distinction between mechanisms (proximate) and reasons (distal). Education, income, and ideology do not cause low TFR directly; they cause delayed marriage, increased contraceptive use, and shorter breastfeeding (or rather, the substitution of formula feeding in contexts where mothers work), which cause low TFR. Bongaarts (2015) updates the original model with adjustments for the post-transition setting; subsequent applications to specific national declines (Ethiopia 2005–2016, India 2015–16, Bangladesh 1996–2011) show varying combinations of the four mechanisms.

解释生育水平差异的标准人口学框架，是 Davis 与 Blake（1956）提出、并由 Bongaarts（1978、2015）以数量形式加以操作化的近端决定因素（proximate determinants）模型。该框架将远端决定因素（社会经济条件、教育水平、文化规范）与近端决定因素（一切远端原因必须经由其起作用的生物—行为机制）区分开来。最初列出的八项近端因素，后被精简为以下四项，可解释跨国 TFR 变异的约 96%：

  1. 处于性结合关系中的女性比例（实质上即：初婚或初次同居的年龄、离婚率、终身未婚率）
  2. 避孕的使用率与有效性
  3. 人工流产（限于合法可及且实际被使用的地区）
  4. 产后不育期（主要由哺乳期长度决定）

这一框架的澄清功用在于：它把「机制」（近端）与「理由」（远端）截然分开。教育、收入、意识形态并不直接造成 TFR 的下降；它们造成的，是更晚的婚姻、更高的避孕使用率、更短的哺乳期（或更准确地说，是在母亲外出工作的语境下以配方奶粉替代母乳）——而这些才直接拉低了 TFR。Bongaarts（2015）针对人口转型完成后的情境对原模型作了调整；后续在埃塞俄比亚（2005–2016）、印度（2015–16）、孟加拉国（1996–2011）等具体下降过程中的应用，显示出这四种机制的不同组合。

Distal determinants: what drives the proximate 远端决定因素：什么驱动了近端

The substantive question is what causes the proximate mechanisms to shift. The literature converges on a small number of distal factors, though their relative weight is debated.

Women's education

The most robust single predictor. Murtin (2013), a regression analysis on time-series data from 1870 to 2000, concludes that "average years of primary schooling among the adult population, rather than income standards, child mortality, or total mortality rates" accounts for 40–80 per cent of the fertility decline as schooling rises from 0 to 7 years. Lutz and Skirbekk (2014) and Lutz (2017) argue that female educational attainment is the single most important determinant of fertility trajectory and that population projections should be educational-attainment-stratified rather than country-stratified. The mechanism operates through several channels: later age at marriage, higher labor-force participation, increased autonomy in fertility decisions, increased contraceptive knowledge, and rising opportunity cost of time spent on childcare.

The cost-of-time mechanism (the "new economics of fertility")

Becker's (1960) household-production theory of fertility predicted that rising women's wages would reduce fertility by raising the opportunity cost of time spent on childcare. The prediction held for the early period of demographic transition. Doepke, Hannusch, Kindermann, and Tertilt (2023), in "The New Economics of Fertility," argue that the simple Becker prediction has broken down: in high-income countries with strong career-family compatibility infrastructure (Sweden, Denmark, France), high female labor-force participation now correlates with higher, not lower, fertility, while countries with weak compatibility infrastructure (Italy, Spain, Japan, South Korea) combine high female education with very low fertility. The relevant variable is not female wages per se but the institutional and cultural compatibility of career and family — measured by childcare availability, parental leave provisions, men's domestic labor share, and workplace flexibility.

Child survival and the quantity–quality tradeoff

Historically, the demographic transition has begun with the decline in child mortality, which removes one major reason for high fertility (insuring against child loss). The Becker (1960) quantity–quality model adds that as parents invest more per child (education, health, opportunity), the marginal cost of an additional child rises, pushing optimal family size down. The historical sequence in most countries: child mortality falls first, fertility falls with a lag of one to several decades, the population goes through a period of rapid growth, and the demographic transition completes when fertility reaches replacement or lower.

Urbanization

Urbanization correlates with reduced fertility through several channels: weaker extended-family support for childcare, higher housing costs, higher costs of urban child-rearing relative to rural, and exposure to lower-fertility reference groups. The relationship is robust but moderate in effect size and partially mediated by education and women's labor-force participation.

Cultural diffusion & ideational change

The "second demographic transition" framework (Lesthaeghe 1995, 2010) argues that classic socio-economic determinants are insufficient to explain post-1970s declines in already-low-fertility countries. The additional driver is ideational: the spread of secularization, individualism, gender-egalitarian norms, and the cultural acceptability of non-traditional family forms. Southeast Asian fertility transitions that occurred before substantial economic development (Knodel et al., on Thailand and Indonesia) provide evidence that ideational diffusion can precede the standard economic preconditions.

Reduced religious influence

Cross-national studies (Skirbekk et al. 2015; the BMC Public Health analysis of 2020) find that religiosity is correlated with higher TFR and with delayed onset of fertility decline, particularly in sub-Saharan Africa and among ultra-orthodox subpopulations within otherwise low-fertility countries (e.g., Israeli Haredim, U.S. Old-Order Anabaptists). The relationship is mediated by educational attainment (more religious populations are often less educated) but a residual effect of religious-doctrinal commitment to procreation appears to persist after controlling for education.

真正的问题在于：是什么使近端机制发生位移？文献在少数几项远端因素上趋于共识，尽管各因素之间的相对权重尚有争论。

女性教育

这是最稳健的单一预测变量。Murtin（2013）在 1870–2000 年的时间序列上做回归分析后得出的结论是：「成人人口的平均初等教育年限——而非收入水平、儿童死亡率或总死亡率——」可解释教育年限由 0 升至 7 年所对应生育率下降的 40–80%。Lutz 与 Skirbekk（2014）以及 Lutz（2017）则进一步主张，女性教育水平是决定生育轨迹最重要的单一因素，人口预测应按教育水平分层，而非按国家分层。其机制由多条路径合成：推迟初婚、提高劳动参与、扩大生育决策的自主、增加避孕知识，并提高用于育儿的时间机会成本。

时间机会成本机制（「新生育经济学」）

Becker（1960）的家庭生产理论曾预言：女性工资上升会通过抬高育儿的时间机会成本而压低生育率。这一预言在人口转型早期是成立的。但 Doepke、Hannusch、Kindermann 与 Tertilt（2023）在〈新生育经济学〉中论证：素朴的 Becker 预言已然失效——在「事业—家庭兼容性」制度基础雄厚的高收入国家（瑞典、丹麦、法国），高女性劳动参与率如今与更高而非更低的生育率正相关；反观兼容性制度薄弱的国家（意大利、西班牙、日本、韩国），则是高女性教育水平与极低生育率并存。真正关键的并不是女性工资本身，而是事业与家庭在制度与文化上的兼容性——以育儿可及性、育儿假政策、男性家务分担比例、工作时间弹性等指标加以衡量。

儿童存活率与「数量—质量」权衡

历史上，人口转型几乎总以儿童死亡率的下降为起点——它去除了高生育的一大主因，即「为防夭折而多生」式的保险动机。Becker（1960）的「数量—质量」模型进一步指出：当父母对每个孩子的投入加大（教育、健康、机会），多生一个孩子的边际成本随之上升，最优家庭规模因而下移。多数国家的历史顺序大抵如此：先是儿童死亡率下降，生育率随后延后数十年方才下降，期间人口经历一段急速增长，待生育率降至更替水平或更低，人口转型遂告完成。

城市化

城市化与低生育率经由多条路径相关：扩展家庭对育儿的支持随之削弱、住房成本攀升、城市育儿的相对成本高于乡村，以及置身于低生育的参照群体之中。这一关系稳健，但效应规模属中等，并部分由教育水平及女性劳动参与率所中介。

文化扩散与观念变迁

Lesthaeghe（1995、2010）提出的「第二次人口转型」框架主张：经典的社会经济因素已不足以解释 1970 年代以来「本已低生育」国家的进一步下滑。其补充驱动力来自观念层面——世俗化、个人主义、性别平等规范的扩散，以及非传统家庭形式的文化可接受性。东南亚那些先于显著经济发展便发生的生育转型（Knodel 等关于泰国与印度尼西亚的研究）便提供了证据：观念扩散完全可以早于标准经济前提条件而启动。

宗教影响的减弱

跨国研究（Skirbekk 等, 2015；BMC Public Health 2020 综述）发现：宗教性与较高的 TFR 相关，亦与生育率下降的延后启动相关——尤其在撒哈拉以南非洲，以及在整体低生育国家内部的极端正统亚群体之中（如以色列的极端正统犹太人、美国旧派再洗礼派）。这一关系部分由教育水平所中介（宗教性较强的人口往往教育程度较低），但即便控制教育之后，「教义层面对生育的承诺」仍残留出可见的效应。

The current global picture 当前全球图景

The United Nations World Population Prospects 2024 (28th revision, October 2024) supplies the current consensus estimates. Headline figures:

  • Global TFR in 2024: 2.2 births per woman, down from 3.3 in 1990 and approximately 4.8 in 1970. The global rate has approximately halved over half a century.
  • 131 of 237 countries and areas (55 per cent) are now below the replacement level of approximately 2.1 births per woman. These countries account for 68 per cent of the global population.
  • Approximately one fifth of countries — including China, Italy, South Korea, Spain, Japan, and several Eastern European states — are at "ultra-low" fertility (under 1.4 births per woman). South Korea's TFR fell below 1.0 in 2022; China reached approximately 1.0 in 2023–2024.
  • The global rate is projected to reach 2.1 (replacement) by 2050 and approximately 1.8 by 2100. The 95 per cent confidence interval for 2100 lies between 1.6 and 2.2.
  • The probability that the global population peaks before 2100 is now estimated at ~80 per cent, with the peak projected for the mid-2080s at approximately 10.3 billion, down from previous (2013) projections that did not anticipate a peak this century.
  • Africa remains the regional exception: TFR of approximately 4.0 in 2024, nearly double the global average. Sub-Saharan Africa drives most projected population growth through 2054.

联合国《世界人口展望 2024》（第 28 次修订版，2024 年 10 月）给出了当前的共识估计。主要数据如下：

  • 2024 年全球 TFR：每位女性 2.2 个孩子，较 1990 年的 3.3、1970 年代的约 4.8 显著走低。全球生育率在半个世纪内大致腰斩。
  • 在 237 个国家与地区中，已有 131 个（即 55%）低于约 2.1 的更替水平。这些国家与地区合计占全球人口的 68%。
  • 约五分之一的国家——其中包括中国、意大利、韩国、西班牙、日本，以及多个东欧国家——已进入「超低生育率」区间（每位女性少于 1.4 个孩子）。韩国 TFR 于 2022 年跌破 1.0；中国则在 2023–2024 年间降至约 1.0。
  • 全球 TFR 预计将在 2050 年前后触及 2.1（更替水平），至 2100 年降至约 1.8。2100 年的 95% 置信区间介于 1.6 与 2.2 之间。
  • 全球人口在 2100 年之前达峰的概率，目前估计约为 80%；峰值预计出现在 2080 年代中期，约 103 亿——相较 2013 年那一版「本世纪不会达峰」的预测，时点已明显前移。
  • 非洲仍是区域性的例外：2024 年 TFR 约 4.0，几近全球平均的两倍。撒哈拉以南非洲将贡献 2054 年之前预期人口增长的绝大部分。

Note: the descriptive does not equal the normative 附注：描述性事实不等于规范性结论

The convergence of two facts — that procreation has been the default trajectory, and that the trajectory is now being widely abandoned — does not by itself constitute an argument for antinatalism. The empirical literature is largely about why people are having fewer children, not about whether they ought to. The reasons that the demographic literature identifies (education, opportunity cost, cost of housing, weak career-family compatibility, ideational change, declining religiosity) are mostly not antinatalist reasons in the philosophical sense. They are reasons specific individuals have for postponing or limiting their own procreation, not reasons grounded in the asymmetry argument, the consent objection, or the harm-imposition framework.

The relationship between the empirical and the normative is one of partial convergence on a normative conclusion reached for unrelated reasons. The philosophical argument against procreation is not strengthened by the fact that fewer people are doing it; the descriptive trend is not weakened by the fact that most of its drivers are non-philosophical. Both can be true simultaneously: the philosophical case for not procreating can be sound, and the empirical decline in procreation can be largely a side-effect of education, economics, and changed norms. The two are independent.

What the empirical literature does establish, however, is that the explanatory burden Overall identified (§6) is not just a philosopher's observation but a documented social structure. The default exists; it is sustained by institutions, policies, and norms; it is currently weakening for largely non-philosophical reasons; and the philosophical critique of it would have had different reception conditions in 1900, in 1970, and in 2024.

两项事实并存——生育曾是默认轨迹、该轨迹正被广泛舍弃——本身并不能凑成一份对反生育主义的论证。经验文献主要回答的是为何人们在少生，而非他们是否应当少生。人口学文献所点明的原因（教育、机会成本、住房成本、孱弱的事业—家庭兼容性、观念变迁、宗教性下降），多数并非哲学意义上的反生育理由——它们是具体个体推迟或限制自身生育时所凭借的理由，而非建立在不对称性论证、同意反驳或伤害施加框架之上的理由。

经验事实与规范结论之间的关系，是「双方各因不相干的理由，部分地汇合于同一结论」。少有人生育这一事实，并不曾强化反生育的哲学论证；多数驱动因素属非哲学性这一点，也未削弱描述性趋势的真实性。两者完全可以同时成立：反生育的哲学论证可能是站得住脚的；而生育率的下降，亦完全可能在很大程度上只是教育、经济、规范变迁的附带产物。二者各行其道。

然而经验文献的确确证了一点：Overall（§6）所揭示的「解释负担」不仅是一位哲学家的洞察，而是一项有据可考的社会结构。默认是真实存在的；它被制度、政策、规范所维系；它当下正因大致非哲学的缘由而松动；而对它的哲学批判，在 1900 年、1970 年、2024 年这三个不同时点上，所面对的接收语境也各不相同。

8 · Objections & Replies 反驳与回应

The non-identity problem 非同一性问题

The strongest principled objection. Derek Parfit, Reasons and Persons (1984) §16, observes: if we change procreative decisions, we change which person comes into existence. The person who would have come into existence under the original choice does not also exist under the alternative; a different person exists, or none. Therefore the original person cannot be worse off in the original world than in the alternative, because in the alternative she does not exist to be compared to.

The challenge for antinatalism: if a person's existence cannot be compared to their non-existence (because there is no subject of comparison), then claims like "this person was harmed by being brought into existence" become metaphysically suspect. Benatar's reply is that the asymmetry argument does not require a comparison between two states of the same subject; it requires only the evaluative truths about the four cells. The absence of pain is good in an impersonal sense, not in the sense of being good for a particular non-existing person.

Whether Benatar's reply succeeds is the central technical debate in the literature. Elizabeth Harman, "Critical Study of Better Never to Have Been" (Noûs, 2009), and Ben Bradley, "Benatar and the Logic of Betterness" (Journal of Ethics & Social Philosophy, 2010), argue that the impersonal reading is unstable. David Boonin, The Non-Identity Problem and the Ethics of Future People (2014), surveys the field.

这是最有原则、也最强劲的一项反驳。Derek Parfit 在《理由与人格》（1984）§16 指出：一旦我们改变生育决策，被带入存在的「是哪个人」也随之改变。原决策下将出现的那一个具体的人，在替代决策下并不同时存在——替代决策下要么是另一个人，要么便没有人。因此，原决策下那个人，在原世界中并不可能比在替代世界中「更糟」——因为在替代世界中，她根本不存在，无从被拿来比较。

这对反生育主义构成的挑战是：若一个人的存在无法与其非存在相比较（因为根本没有可供比较的主体），那么诸如「此人因被带入存在而受了害」之类的说法，在形而上学层面便显得可疑。Benatar 的回应是：不对称性论证并不要求在「同一主体的两种状态」之间作比较；它所要求的，只是关于四个格子的价值评判。痛苦的缺席，是在非人称的意义上为善——而非在「对某个并不存在的具体之人而言为善」的意义上为善。

Benatar 的回应能否站稳，正是文献中的核心技术争议。Elizabeth Harman〈对《永不存在更好》的批判性研究〉（Noûs, 2009）以及 Ben Bradley〈Benatar 与「更好」之逻辑〉（JESP, 2010）都论证：这种非人称解读并不稳固。David Boonin《非同一性问题与未来人之伦理》（2014）则对整个领域作了综述。

The "life is worth living" reply 「人生值得活」反驳

The most common informal reply: most people, when asked, judge their lives to be on balance good and would not prefer to have never existed. Therefore the asymmetry argument's premise that life is on balance bad is empirically false; therefore the conclusion does not follow.

The standard antinatalist replies are three. First, Benatar argues that the self-assessment is systematically biased — by Pollyanna-effect optimism bias, by adaptation effects, by comparison to imagined worse counterfactuals, and by the unavailability of the non-existence comparison class. People's reports of life-satisfaction are not a reliable measure of life's actual quality. Second, even if life is on balance good for the existing person, the asymmetry argument does not require it to be bad; it requires only that the absence of pleasure (in the non-existence case) is not bad. Third, Shiffrin-style consent-based arguments do not require life to be on balance bad at all; they require only that life contains serious harms imposed without consent.

The dispute over Pollyanna-effect bias is empirically tractable in principle but contested in practice. See Daniel Kahneman's work on experienced versus remembered utility, which complicates the assumption that self-reported life satisfaction tracks actual welfare.

这是最常见的一种非形式反驳：多数人若被问及，会判断自己的人生整体而言是好的，并不希望自己从未存在。因此，不对称性论证「人生整体为坏」这一前提在经验上为假，其结论也就站不住脚。

反生育方对此通常有三层回应。其一，Benatar 论证：此种自我评价存在系统性的偏差——「波丽安娜式」乐观偏差（Pollyanna effect）、适应效应、与想象中更糟反事实的对照，以及「非存在」这一比较类根本无从获得。人对自己生命满意度的报告，并非衡量生命实际质量的可靠尺度。其二，纵然对已存在者而言其生命整体上是好的，不对称性论证也并不要求其为坏；它只要求快乐之缺席（在非存在情形里）并不为坏。其三，Shiffrin 一类基于同意的论证根本不要求生命整体上是坏的；它只要求生命中含有未经同意而被施加的严重伤害。

关于「波丽安娜偏差」之争，原则上可由经验加以处理，实际操作中却颇有争论。参见 Kahneman 关于「当下感受效用」与「记忆效用」之分的研究——它使「自报生命满意度真实追踪福祉」这一假设变得不那么牢靠。

The evolutionary debunking objection 演化拆穿式反驳

Reproduction is the central activity of biological life. The capacity to find reproduction valuable is, by selection, deeply embedded in human psychology. An evolutionary debunker might argue that the antinatalist's contrary intuitions are themselves products of selection-relaxed environments or of pathological cognitive states; the pro-natalist intuitions, by contrast, are the ones that have been vindicated by 3.5 billion years of biological success.

The antinatalist reply has two parts. First, the genetic etiology of an intuition does not settle its rational standing — that would commit the genetic fallacy. Selection vindicates whatever conduces to reproduction; it does not vindicate the rational permissibility of that conduct. Second, the same debunking move applies symmetrically: pro-natalist intuitions are also evolutionarily produced and thus equally suspect; the question of which intuitions to credit must be settled on grounds other than their evolutionary provenance.

The deeper version of this objection — that there is no Archimedean point outside biology from which to assess biology's products — is a real challenge to all of normative ethics, not specifically to antinatalism.

繁殖是生物生命的核心活动。「将繁殖视为有价值」的能力，经由自然选择，深植于人类心理。一种「演化拆穿式」反驳可能这样说：反生育者所持的反向直觉，本身只是「选择压力松弛的环境」或某些病态认知状态的产物；而亲生育的直觉则相反，乃是经由 35 亿年生物成功所验证过的。

反生育方的回应有两层。其一，一条直觉的发生学起源并不决定它在理性上是否站得住——否则便犯了发生学谬误。自然选择验证一切有助于繁殖之事，却并不验证此种行为在理性上是否可被允许。其二，同一种拆穿手法可对称地反过来用：亲生育的直觉同样是演化的产物，因而同样可疑；要决定该相信哪一类直觉，必须诉诸演化来源之外的根据。

这一反驳的更深层版本——即根本不存在某个「位于生物学之外的阿基米德点」可供评判生物学之产物——实际上是对整个规范伦理学的真正挑战，而不是专门冲反生育主义而来。

The "would you kill yourself, then?" objection 「那么你为何不自杀？」反驳

A common rhetorical move: if non-existence is preferable to existence, why does the antinatalist continue to live? The objection assumes that not procreating and committing suicide are evaluatively equivalent.

They are not. Benatar (2006, ch. 7) is explicit: it is a category mistake to infer pro-mortalism from antinatalism. The reasons against bringing a new existence into being are different from the reasons against ending an existence already underway. An ongoing life has formed interests, attachments, projects, relationships; ending it is a serious harm to the existing subject and a serious harm to those connected to her. A potential life has none of these; refusing to start it is no harm to anyone. The asymmetry of the two acts is roughly the asymmetry of cells (3) and (4) in figure 2.

Schopenhauer makes the same point: suicide is the affirmation of will (the will to escape suffering is still will); the proper response is the calm refusal to renew the will in others. The recurring pessimist tradition (Schopenhauer, Mainländer's biographical exception aside, Cioran, Zapffe) holds that ongoing life is to be lived out with whatever grace is available, while procreation is the act that has actual normative weight.

The skiing analogy serves the same purpose: "I went to a place; the experience is not worth recommending. Why am I not leaving? Because I am already here, and while I am here I will ski well. But I will not come again, and I will not tell anyone to come." The two clauses are independent.

一种常见的修辞式反驳是：既然非存在优于存在，反生育者为何还在继续活着？此反驳暗中假定：不生育与自杀在评价上是等同的。

它们并不等同。Benatar（2006, 第 7 章）清楚指出：从反生育推出促死主义，是一种范畴错误。「反对把一个新存在带入存在」的理由，与「反对终止一段已在进行的存在」的理由，根本属于不同的范畴。一段已在进行的生命，已经形成了利益、依恋、计划、关系；终止它，无论对当事人自身还是对与她相连的人，都是严重的伤害。一段潜在的生命则一无所有；拒绝开启它，并不伤害任何人。两种行为之间的不对称，大致对应于图 2 中第 (3)、(4) 两格之间的不对称。

叔本华亦持同样的立场：自杀其实是意志的肯定——「欲逃离痛苦的意志」仍然是意志；恰当的回应是平静地拒绝在他者身上重启这一意志。整个反复回响的悲观主义传统（叔本华；Mainländer 的传记例外暂且搁置；Cioran；Zapffe）皆是这样的态度：在世之生，便以尚可获得的从容把它过完；而真正具有规范分量的行为，是生育。

滑雪的类比正落在此处：「我去了一个地方；那里的体验不值得推荐。我为何不离开？因为我既已在此，便在此好好滑一会。但我不会再来，也不会告诉任何人来。」这两句之间，相互独立。

The "if everyone did this, humanity would end" objection 「若众人皆如此，则人类绝灭」反驳

A Kantian universalizability objection: if the maxim "do not procreate" were universalized, the species would go extinct; the extinction of the species is bad; therefore the maxim cannot be universalized; therefore it is not a permissible maxim.

The antinatalist replies vary. Some accept the conclusion of human extinction and deny that it is bad: the cessation of a species that produces the suffering at issue is, on this view, not a bad outcome but the natural completion of the antinatalist argument. The Voluntary Human Extinction Movement (see §9) is the explicit form. Others argue that the objection conflates an individual normative claim with a collective coordination problem: a single act of non-procreation has a vanishingly small effect on aggregate fertility, and the assessment of the act does not depend on counterfactual universal compliance. Still others (Cabrera) point out that the Kantian framework's claim that species extinction is intrinsically bad is itself unargued.

The deeper version of this exchange concerns whether non-existence of a sentient species is a loss in any sense that matters. If the answer is "no" (because there is no subject of the loss, as in the asymmetry argument), then the universalizability objection has no bite.

这是一项康德式的普遍化反驳：假若「不要生育」这一格言被普遍化，物种将归于灭绝；物种灭绝为坏；故此格言不可被普遍化；故它不是一项可被允许的格言。

反生育方的回应路数不一。有的人径直接受「人类绝灭」这一结论，并否认它是「坏」的：在他们看来，那个不断产生上述痛苦的物种之停止，并非什么糟糕的结局，而是反生育论证本身自然走完的终点。「自愿人类灭绝运动」（见 §9）便是这一回应的明确形态。另一些人则指出此反驳混淆了「个体规范主张」与「集体协调问题」：单独一次「不生育」对总生育率的影响微乎其微，对该行为的评判并不依赖于「假设所有人都照做」这一反事实。还有一些人（如 Cabrera）则直接反问：康德框架中所预设的「物种绝灭本身就是坏」这一命题，本身是不曾被论证的。

这一交锋更深的版本所触及的，是这样一个问题：一个有感知能力的物种之不存在，是否在任何有意义的意义上算得上是一种「损失」？若答案是「否」（因为如不对称性论证所示，根本没有一个「承受这一损失」的主体），那么普遍化反驳便没有真正的咬合点。

The "but new experiences are valuable" reply 「但新经验是有价值的」反驳

A pro-natalist line of reply, sometimes called the experience-machine inversion: even granting that life contains harms, the goods of existence — love, knowledge, beauty, accomplishment — are themselves valuable and could not exist without the existence of the beings that experience them. To refuse to procreate is to refuse to bring these goods into the world.

The antinatalist reply, again, is the asymmetry. The world without the new being is not deprived of the goods, because there is no subject of deprivation. The pro-natalist argument has rhetorical force because we imagine the not-yet-existent child as a kind of waiting presence whose chance at goods we are denying. But this is precisely the projection that the asymmetry argument is designed to dissolve: the non-existent has no chance to be denied.

A subsidiary observation: if the value of new experiences could justify creating new beings, the same value could justify forcibly creating new beings in all available niches, since each new being would bring new goods into the world. This conclusion is universally rejected, which suggests that the principle being invoked is doing less work than it appears.

一条亲生育的回应进路，或可称之为体验机器反演：纵然承认生命含有伤害，存在所带来的种种善——爱、知识、美、成就——本身仍是有价值的；倘若没有体验者的存在，这些善便也无所存放。于是「拒绝生育」便等同于「拒绝把这些善带入世间」。

反生育方的回应依然出自不对称性：没有新生命的世界并不曾被「剥夺」这些善，因为根本就没有谁在那里被剥夺。亲生育论证之所以颇具说服力，是因为我们不自觉地把那个尚未存在的孩子，想象为某种「在彼处等候着的人」，仿佛我们正在剥夺他的善之机会。但这恰是不对称性论证所要消解的投射：非存在者并没有什么机会可被剥夺。

另有一条附带观察：倘若「新经验的价值」足以为「创造新存在者」辩护，那么同一价值便也足以为「在一切可用生态位中强行创造新存在者」辩护——因为每一个新生者都会为世界带来新的善。可此一推论几乎被所有人拒斥，这反过来表明：所援引的那条原则，实际承担的工作远不及其字面所示。

9 · Adjacent Positions 邻近立场

Promortalism 促死主义

The view that, for at least some existing sentient beings, it would be better to die than to continue existing. Promortalism is logically independent of antinatalism: one can hold either without the other. Hegesias of Cyrene is the historical exemplar; in contemporary philosophy, the view is rarely defended in pure form, though it lurks in some readings of Mainländer. The standard antinatalist position explicitly rejects the promortalist inference (see §8).

这一立场主张：对至少某些现存有感知能力的主体而言，死亡好过继续存在。促死主义与反生育主义在逻辑上互相独立——人可以只持其一，而不持另一。昔兰尼的赫格西亚是历史范例；当代哲学少有以纯粹形式为之辩护者，虽然它在某些对 Mainländer 的解读中若隐若现。标准反生育立场则明确拒绝由反生育向促死主义推进（见 §8）。

Efilism Efilism（生命反转主义）

An online-philosophy radicalization of antinatalism, coined by the YouTube philosopher Inmendham (Gary Mosher). The position extends antinatalism to all sentient life: not merely human procreation should cease, but the entire biosphere — insofar as it produces suffering — should cease. The term is "life" spelled backward. Efilism is rarely engaged with by academic philosophers but has a real online following; its relationship to mainstream antinatalism is the same as the relationship between any radicalized internet position and its more sober academic counterpart.

这是反生育主义在网络哲学中的一种激进化形态，由 YouTube 哲学家 Inmendham（Gary Mosher）所造。该立场把反生育延及一切有感知能力的生命：不只人类应停止生育，整个生物圈——只要它在生产痛苦——皆应停止。其名 Efilism，正是 life 的倒写。学院哲学家鲜少与之对话，但它在网络上确有一群追随者；它与主流反生育主义的关系，类似于任何被网络激进化的立场与其更冷静的学术对应物之间的关系。

Voluntary Human Extinction Movement (VHEMT) 自愿人类灭绝运动（VHEMT）

Founded by Les U. Knight in 1991. Slogan: "May we live long and die out." Argues that the voluntary cessation of human reproduction is the most ethical response to the harm humanity inflicts on other species and ecosystems. VHEMT is misanthropic-antinatalist in Benatar's typology (focused on harm by humans rather than harm to the prospective humans), and is more activist than scholarly. The movement explicitly distances itself from coercion: it advocates voluntary non-reproduction, not population control by force.

由 Les U. Knight 于 1991 年创立。其口号为：「愿我们长生而后绝。」该运动主张：自愿停止人类的繁殖，乃是对「人类施加于其他物种与生态系统的伤害」最具伦理性的回应。在 Benatar 的类型学里，VHEMT 属于厌世式反生育——其重心在人类所施之害，而不在人类所受之害；它的气质亦更近于行动主义而非学术。该运动明确与一切强制手段划清界限：它倡导的是自愿不育，而非以强力实施的人口控制。

Childfree movement 自愿无子运动

A sociological category for adults who choose not to have children. The childfree position is a lifestyle stance rather than a philosophical thesis: it makes no claim about whether others should procreate, only about the chooser's own life. The distinction from antinatalism is real and important — most childfree people are not antinatalists, and the conflation in popular writing tends to weaken both. The literature includes Laura S. Scott, Two Is Enough (2009); Amy Blackstone, Childfree by Choice (2019); the documentary My So-Called Selfish Life (2021).

这是一个社会学范畴，用以指代那些自主选择不育子女的成年人。自愿无子是一种生活方式上的立场，而非一项哲学命题：它并不主张「他人是否也该生育」，只关涉选择者自身的生命。它与反生育主义之间的区分既真实又重要——大多数自愿无子者并非反生育主义者；通俗书写中两者的混同，往往令双方都被弱化。相关文献包括 Laura S. Scott《两个就够》（2009）、Amy Blackstone《自愿无子》（2019），以及纪录片《我所谓自私的人生》（2021）。

Longtermism & the population-ethics tension 长期主义与人口伦理学张力

The longtermist position within effective altruism (William MacAskill, What We Owe the Future, 2022) holds that bringing into existence very large numbers of future flourishing beings is one of the most morally important things present generations can do. This is the structural opposite of antinatalism. The disagreement turns on the same asymmetry: longtermism treats absent flourishing as a loss; Benatar treats it as no loss. Population ethics as a subfield exists in part because these two views generate mathematically incompatible conclusions about the value of large future populations.

Parfit's repugnant conclusion (a world of many barely-worth-living lives is better than a world of fewer flourishing lives, under standard total utilitarian aggregation) is the formal point at which both sides meet: neither longtermism nor antinatalism accepts the repugnant conclusion as ordinarily formulated, but they avoid it in opposite directions.

有效利他主义阵营内的长期主义立场（William MacAskill《我们对未来的责任》, 2022）主张：把数量极其庞大的未来繁荣存在者带入存在，是当下世代在道德上最重要的事情之一。这一立场在结构上正是反生育主义的对立面。两者的分歧仍系于同一道不对称：长期主义将「未在场的繁荣」视作一种损失；Benatar 则视之为根本没有损失。人口伦理学之所以会作为一个独立子领域存在，部分原因便在于这两种观点对「庞大未来人口的价值」竟得出了数学上互不相容的结论。

Parfit 的令人厌恶的结论（在标准的全总和功利主义聚合下，一个由众多「刚刚值得活」的生命所组成的世界，反倒优于一个由更少「繁荣生命」组成的世界），正是两方在形式上相遇的节点：长期主义与反生育主义在通常表述下都不接受这一结论——但两者对它的躲避方向恰恰相反。
